Interested in joining the lab?

The Jain Research Laboratory is always looking for talented students to join our research team.

Prospective Graduate Students:

Prof. Jain receives multiple requests a day from students interested in joining her group. However, she will only consider students who have already applied to Purdue University. If you have applied, please state that you "have applied to the School of Mechanical Engineering" at the beginning of your e-mail. If you have applied to a different School at Purdue, please include that information in your e-mail.

Purdue University Undergraduate Students:

Please email Prof. Jain a statement of your research interests, an unofficial transcript, and a current resumé. This information will facilitate a discussion of possible opportunities for you in the JRL. While there is no minimum required GPA to be considered for an undergraduate research position, students who have earned an A- or better in relevant courses (ME 200, ME 300, ME 365, ME 375, and any math courses) are generally most successful in the group.
